What to fix first

Methodology

  • Headers: HTTP response header presence and quality checks, with a weighted letter grade.
  • TLS: Certificate dates, subject/issuer, SANs, negotiated protocol; no CA validation performed in demo.
  • Ports: Async TCP connect scan across a curated list; no banner grabbing.
  • Subdomains: Certificate Transparency enumeration plus lightweight DNS resolution.
  • DNS: A/AAAA/MX/TXT, SPF/DMARC presence and policy.
  • Takeovers: Heuristic signatures for common providers (best-effort).
Note: Demo data is illustrative. Always scan your own domain with authorization.

Glossary

  • CSP (Content-Security-Policy): Browser policy to limit what can execute.
  • HSTS: Strict HTTPS enforcement; preload submits your domain to browsers’ preload lists.
  • SPF/DMARC: Email authentication records that help prevent spoofing.
  • Takeover: A dangling DNS pointing to a 3rd-party provider with no active site.
